About Manga Autos

Who We Are

Abuja's Most Trusted Luxury Auto Dealer

Founded in 2018, Manga Autos was built on a single conviction: Nigerian car buyers deserved the same transparency, professionalism, and excellence that luxury auto buyers in Europe and America take for granted.

We source directly from certified auctions in the USA, Germany, UK, and UAE — eliminating the layers of middlemen that inflate prices and obscure vehicle histories. Every car we list has been personally inspected, documented, and verified by our team before it reaches our customers.

Today, 247+ satisfied clients across Abuja, Lagos, and Port Harcourt stand as testament to our commitment. Many return for their second, third, and fourth vehicles — because when you experience the Manga Autos difference, there is no going back.
